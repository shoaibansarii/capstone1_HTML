18) Semantic Tags

  • Provides meaningful structure of web content
  • Enhances SEO, accessibility

Commonly Used Semantic Tags

  1. <header>: to represent top section of webpage (headings, logos, navigation, intro content)
  2. <footer>: footer info (copyright info, contact details)
  3. <nav>: holds navigation menu and links
  4. <main>: signifies main content area of a webpage
  5. <article>: encapsulated self-contained content (news article, blog post)
  6. <section>: thematic grouping of content
  7. <aside>: sidebars/content related to main content
  8. <figure and <figcaption>: embed images,charts,diagrams along with caption
  9. <time>: to represent time related info (dates, times)
