HTML Quotation Tag

To semantically mark-up quotations thereby providing semantic meaning, readability and SEO benefits

<blockquote>

  • for long, block-level quotes
  • browsers indent <blockquote> elements

<q>

  • for short, inline quotes
  • browsers insert quotation marks around quotation

'cite' attribute

Specifies URL of the quote's source
