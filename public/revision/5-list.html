5) List Element

Web Developers use lists to neatly display data

Three types:-

  1. Ordered List: <ol>...<li>...</li>...</ol>
  2. Unordered List: <ul>...<li>...</li>...</ul>
  3. Definition List: <dl><dt>.....</dt><dd>.....</dd></dl>

Attributes

  • For UL: type="square/circle", by default=disc
  • For OL: type="I/i/a/A" , start="3"
